Spaghetti e Aglio Olio

Description

Recipe for delicious and original Spaghetti e Aglio Olio.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Toast the garlic in olive oil.
  3. Stir the seasonings into the pasta, then stir in the garlic and oil.
  4. Toss with cheese.

And that's it. Enjoy your Meal!
